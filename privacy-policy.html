Privacy Policy

This Privacy Statement outlines the policies of **CP Star LLC** (hereinafter referred to as "the Company," "we," "us," or "our") regarding the collection, use, and protection of your personal information when you agree to receive SMS/MMS messages from us. This statement is provided to ensure transparency and to comply with requirements set forth by Mobile Network Operators.

Information We Collect

When you consent to receive SMS/MMS messages from us, we may collect the following information:

  • **Mobile Phone Number:** Your mobile phone number that you provide to us for receiving messages.
  • **Message Content:** The content of the SMS/MMS messages we send to you and any replies you may send back to us.
  • **Delivery Information:** Information about whether our messages were successfully delivered to your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through SMS/MMS messaging for the following purposes:

  • **Operational Communications:** To send you important information related to our freight brokerage services, such as load details, dispatch updates, status notifications, and other relevant operational information.
  • **Customer Support:** To respond to your inquiries or requests submitted via SMS/MMS.
  • **Service Improvement:** To analyze message delivery and engagement to improve the effectiveness of our communication.

Important Privacy Commitment: No Sharing or Selling for Marketing Purposes

We want to explicitly assure you that your personal information, specifically your mobile phone number and message content, will NEVER be shared with or sold to third parties for their marketing purposes. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your information from unwanted marketing solicitations from other companies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the confidentiality and security of your mobile phone number and message content. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over mobile networks or electronic storage is completely secure.

Opt-Out Instructions

You can withdraw your consent to receive SMS/MMS messages from us at any time. To stop receiving messages, please follow the opt-out instructions provided in our messages (e.g., replying with "STOP"). Once you opt-out, you will no longer receive SMS/MMS messages from us, except for a confirmation message if applicable.

Data Retention

We will retain your mobile phone number and message records only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Statement and to comply with any applicable legal or regulatory requirements.
